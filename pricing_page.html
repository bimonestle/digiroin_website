Your developing teams are increasingly bringing in new senior developers and providers who sometimes may not have firsthand experience playing the network effect game. We have highlighted two types of network effect distributions- API and On Premise. Our tidy API is used to build a collective prediction machine. On Premise data is used to power it to produce your own predictions.
